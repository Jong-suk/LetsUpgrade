Avengers Cast

Robert Downey Jr

Chris Hemsworth

Chris Hemsworth played the role of 'Thor Odinson' portrayed as 'The God Of Thunder' who is from another world called 'Asgard'.

Mark Alan Ruffalo played the role of 'Bruce Banner' or more popularly known as 'Hulk' in the Marvel Series 'Avengers', who was a Gamma Radiation Researcher and Scientist, and got his powers due to a huge accident in one of the projects.

Scarlett Johansson

Scarlett Johansson played the role of 'Natasha Romanoff' or more popularly known as 'Black Widow' in the Marvel Series 'Avengers', who is highly trained former-KGB assassin and a former agent of S.H.I.E.L.D.

Jeremy Lee Renner

Jeremy Lee Renner played the role of 'Clint Barton' or more popularly known as 'Hawkeye' in the Marvel Series 'Avengers', who is a master archer and a former agent of S.H.I.E.L.D.
